# Model for Superconducting Tc of High Pressure Hydrides
<!--benchmark_description--> - Description: This is a benchmark to evaluate how accurately an AI model can predict the superconducting transition temperature of hydride materials under pressure. The dataset contains the structures and DFT calculated Tc of the hydride materials under various amounts of pressure. Here we use mean absolute error (MAE) to compare models with respect to DFT accuracy.
